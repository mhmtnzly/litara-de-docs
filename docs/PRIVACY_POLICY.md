# Privacy Policy — Litara Deutsch

**Effective date:** April 26, 2026
**Last updated:** April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Litara Deutsch** mobile application
("Litara Deutsch", "the app", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares
information about you when you use our service.

By using Litara Deutsch, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you provide directly
- **Account information**: when you sign in or create an account, we may
  receive your email address (via Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In).
- **Learning preferences**: chosen CEFR level (A0–C1), preferred narrator,
  selected books — stored locally and optionally synced to your account.
- **Subscription status**: managed through RevenueCat / Google Play Billing.

### 1.2 Information collected automatically
- **Usage analytics**: anonymized event data via Firebase Analytics
  (book opens, chapter progress, session duration, crashes).
- **Device data**: device model, OS version, app version, language, country
  code, network type. Used to debug issues and improve compatibility.
- **Advertising identifiers**: Google Advertising ID (Android) for AdMob ads.
- **Crash reports**: stack traces and device state via Firebase Crashlytics.

### 1.3 Information we do NOT collect
- We do NOT collect microphone audio, contacts, photos, location, SMS, or
  call history.
- We do NOT sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide audiobook playback and reading features.
- Track your reading progress so you can resume where you left off.
- Personalize content recommendations within the app.
- Process subscription purchases via Google Play Billing & RevenueCat.
- Display advertisements via Google AdMob (when you do not have an active
  subscription).
- Improve the app's stability, performance, and usability.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Data Storage and Retention

- **On-device data** (reading progress, preferences, cached audio): stored
  locally on your device. Cleared when you uninstall the app.
- **Server-side data** (analytics, crash reports): retained by Firebase
  according to Google's standard retention periods, typically 14–60 months.
- **Subscription records**: retained as long as required by Google Play and
  applicable tax/legal regulations.

You may request deletion of your account-linked data at any time by
contacting us (see Section 9).

---

## 5. Children's Privacy

Litara Deutsch is intended for users **age 13 and older**. We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you
believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and
we will delete it.

---

## 6. Advertising

The free tier of Litara Deutsch displays ads provided by Google AdMob.
Ads may be personalized using your Google Advertising ID. You can:
- Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings:
  *Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of ads
  personalization*.
- Remove ads entirely by purchasing a Litara Deutsch subscription.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom,
Switzerland, or California (USA), you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** — request a copy of your personal data.
- **Right to rectification** — correct inaccurate personal data.
- **Right to erasure** — request deletion of your personal data.
- **Right to restriction** — limit how we process your data.
- **Right to data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable
  format.
- **Right to object** — object to data processing for marketing or analytics.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — at any time, where consent is the legal
  basis.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in
Section 9.
