English

This Privacy Policy describes how the Litara Deutsch mobile application ("Litara Deutsch", "the app", "we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our service. By using Litara Deutsch, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information — when you sign in or create an account, we may receive your email address (via Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In).
  • Learning preferences — chosen CEFR level (A0–C1), preferred narrator, selected books — stored locally and optionally synced to your account.
  • Subscription status — managed through RevenueCat / Google Play Billing.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage analytics — anonymized event data via Firebase Analytics (book opens, chapter progress, session duration, crashes).
  • Device data — device model, OS version, app version, language, country code, network type.
  • Advertising identifiers — Google Advertising ID (Android) for AdMob ads.
  • Crash reports — stack traces and device state via Firebase Crashlytics.

Information we do NOT collect: microphone audio, contacts, photos, location, SMS, or call history. We do NOT sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide audiobook playback and reading features, track your reading progress, personalize content recommendations, process subscription purchases via Google Play Billing & RevenueCat, display advertisements via Google AdMob (when you do not have an active subscription), improve the app's stability and performance, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Storage and Retention

On-device data (reading progress, preferences, cached audio) is stored locally and cleared when you uninstall the app. Server-side data (analytics, crash reports) is retained by Firebase according to Google's standard retention periods, typically 14–60 months. Subscription records are retained as long as required by Google Play and applicable tax/legal regulations. You may request deletion of your account-linked data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Litara Deutsch is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Advertising

The free tier of Litara Deutsch displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Ads may be personalized using your Google Advertising ID. You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID / Opt out of ads personalization) or remove ads entirely by purchasing a Litara Deutsch subscription.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, Switzerland, or California (USA), you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data, as well as to object to processing or withdraw consent. Contact us at schuivwandwereld@gmail.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures (HTTPS, token-based authentication, encrypted storage at our Cloudflare R2 origin) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
